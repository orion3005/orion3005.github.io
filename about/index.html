Someone that is on a journey, unsure of the destination,

taking it one day at a time.

Chasing clarity, craving peace, but learning to embrace the chaos.

He feels everything yet says nothing.

He wants to speak, but his voice goes missing.

He wants connection, but he guards the door unsure whether to open it.

He wears a stone-cold face

while his mind fights battles no one sees.

Calm on the outside, but a war rages within.

He's trying to grow without losing himself.

He's learning to stay soft, without getting stepped on.

He's not just surviving. He's trying to live.
